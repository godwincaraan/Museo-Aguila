Museo Aguila

Museo Aguila is a an art gallery developed by the students of BCS13 from DLSU-D. This aims to showcase the talent of the creators and how art help them to travel freely despite of the current situation. This collection composes of traditional, digital arts and photgraphs will bring its audience to the magical word of the artists.
