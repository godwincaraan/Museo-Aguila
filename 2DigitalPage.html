Reaching the Light

BY JHUMAR GODWIN CARAAN

The tiny hands that form roots symbolize the ecological system, and the big hand that shapes a tree's trunk represents me. Just like the function of roots in a tree, the ecological system nurtures and develops me. Whereas it helps me to grow from an infant that is too innocent to an enlightened teenager. Enlightenment leads me to use my senses positively and helpfully. An eye that can recognize the needs and never become blind to the wrong things that my fellows and I have done. An ear that is not deaf to the voice and critiques by others. A mouth that is not afraid to speak up for the sake of me, my loved ones, and my country. A heart that cares and loves the people that surround me. Lastly, an open-minded and conscious mind to anything. In conclusion, the ecological system is the reason behind my behavior and responses.

Salvation of All Creation

BY JHUMAR GODWIN CARAAN

This artwork concludes that Jesus is the savior of all humankind and serve as the way on showing the love of God to its creation. Also, Jesus becomes one of our inspirations and our companions in our daily lives. Let us all remember that we are not alone in conquering the darkness in our lives because God and Jesus are always in our side and always ready to heal our wounds.
